# Requirements: Zürcher Unterland Räumungen

## Business Information

**Company:** Zürcher Unterland Räumungen
**Industry:** House Clearance, Waste Disposal, Decluttering (Räumung, Entsorgung, Entrümpelung)

**Value Proposition:**
- Discreet, fast, and eco-friendly house clearances
- Fixed price guarantee (Festpreisgarantie) - no hidden costs
- Complete service from furniture disposal to final sweeping

**Target Audience:**
- Families dealing with inheritance (Erbfall)
- Seniors moving to care homes
- Landlords needing quick clearance

**Geographic Focus:** Dielsdorf, Bülach, Regensdorf, Steinmaur (Bezirk Dielsdorf)

---

## Brand Guidelines

**Primary Color:** Trust Blue (#0B4F6C)
**Background:** White/Beige (#FFFFFF / #F5F5F0)
**Avoid:** Black, Red (looks like danger/trash)

**Typography:**
- Font: Inter (readable sans-serif)
- Large sizes for older target audience

**Tone:** Empathetic, Efficient, Serious, Clean (not loud/aggressive)

---

## Single Page Structure

### 1. Navbar
- Logo: Zürcher Unterland Räumungen
- Links: Leistungen, Ablauf, Über uns, Kontakt
- CTA: "Jetzt anfragen"

### 2. Hero Section
- Headline: "Sorgenfreie Wohnungsräumung im Zürcher Unterland"
- Subtext: Diskreter, schneller Service mit Festpreisgarantie
- CTA: "Gratis Besichtigungstermin"
- Image: Clean empty room (professional, not trashy)

### 3. Trust Badges
- "Besenrein-Garantie" - Spotless handover guaranteed
- "100% Recycling" - Eco-friendly disposal
- "Festpreis" - Fixed price, no surprises

### 4. Services (4 cards)
1. Wohnungsräumung - Complete apartment clearance
2. Entsorgung - Professional disposal
3. Kellerräumung - Basement/cellar clearance
4. Firmenauflösung - Business dissolution

### 5. Process (3 steps)
1. Besichtigung (Gratis) - Free on-site assessment
2. Offerte - Written quote within 24h
3. Räumung & Übergabe - Clearance and handover

### 6. About Section
- "Lokales Familienunternehmen aus Dielsdorf"
- Trust-building story vs anonymous national chains

### 7. Testimonials (2 quotes)
Focus themes: Reliable, On time, Friendly

### 8. Contact Section
- Large phone number (click-to-call)
- Form: Name, Phone, Object type, Preferred date
- Address: Dielsdorf area

### 9. Footer
- Logo + tagline
- Quick links
- Contact info
- Copyright
